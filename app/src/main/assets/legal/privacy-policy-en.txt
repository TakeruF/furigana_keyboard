Furigana Keyboard Privacy Policy
Effective: July 10, 2026

1. Provider and contact
Furigana Keyboard is provided by the Furigana Keyboard Project. Privacy inquiries may be submitted through:
https://github.com/TakeruF/furigana_keyboard/issues

2. Information collected
The app developer does not access, collect, or transmit typed text, handwriting strokes, personal information, device identifiers, usage data, or location. The app has no account, advertising, or analytics features.

3. Input processing
Handwriting strokes and composing text are processed temporarily on the device to recognize characters and show candidates. The app does not retain input history. Text that you confirm is delivered through Android's input connection to the app currently receiving keyboard input. That receiving app's privacy policy governs its subsequent handling of the text.

4. Network communication
The app does not request Android's INTERNET permission and does not contact an external server for recognition, dictionary lookup, or settings.

5. On-device storage
The app stores preferences such as display language, reading style, continuous handwriting, and haptic feedback on the device. The recognition model and dictionaries are also stored locally. Input content and handwriting history are not stored. App backup is disabled.

6. Permissions and access
Android grants the app access to the input field in its role as an input method. The app does not request sensitive runtime permissions for the camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, files, or advertising identifiers.

7. Retention and deletion
Preferences remain until you change them, clear app data in Android settings, or uninstall the app. Because typed text and handwriting strokes are not retained by the app, there is no server-held input data to request for deletion.

8. Third-party materials
The app uses Zinnia, the Tegaki Japanese model, KANJIDIC2, JMdict, AndroidX, Material Components, and Kotlin. They operate locally in this app and do not transmit user data from it. Sources and licenses are available under Settings > More > About & licenses.

9. Changes
If app functionality or data handling changes, this policy and the in-app disclosures will be updated. Material changes will be communicated by an appropriate method.

This policy describes the current fully offline version of the app.
